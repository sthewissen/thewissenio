Enabling for an entire app at once

Adding DebugRainbows to your app is as simple as installing the NuGet package and adding the IsDebug="true" attached property. However, if your app has a lot of pages, you might not want to go through the effort of adding the attached property to every single page. Luckily we can use a Style to get around this!
